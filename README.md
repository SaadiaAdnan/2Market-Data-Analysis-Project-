2Market-Data-Analysis-Project-
______________________________

This project analyses customer purchasing behaviour for 2Market, a global supermarket operating across in‑store and online channels. 

The aim is to uncover data-driven insights that support targeted marketing strategies, improve campaign effectiveness, and identify high-value customer segments.


🎯 Objectives
________________

- Analyse how demographics (age, income, marital status, education, country) influence spending  
- Identify high-revenue product categories  
- Evaluate advertising channel effectiveness (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Bulkmail, Brochure)  
- Support targeted marketing and budget allocation decisions  


📊 Key Insights
__________________

- Majority of customers are aged **40–60** with an average age of **44**, indicating a middle-aged customer base  
- **Spending increases with age**, with customers aged 60+ contributing the highest average spend  
- **Higher education levels correlate with higher spending**, indicating strong high-value segments  
- **Alcohol and non-vegetarian products generate the highest revenue**, highlighting key product drivers  
- **Spain is the strongest market**, with high customer base, spending, and advertising exposure  
- **Digital marketing channels (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook)** significantly outperform brochures  
- Approximately **20% of customers respond to advertisements**, with variation across demographics and regions  


📈 Business Recommendations
_____________________________

- Increase investment in **high-performing digital channels** (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook)  
- Reduce spending on **low-performing brochure campaigns**  
- Target **high-value demographic groups** (married, middle-aged, educated customers)  
- Focus on **high-performing markets (Spain, SA, CA)**  
- Develop tailored strategies for **low-exposure markets (AUS, IND, US, ME)**  
- Use A/B testing to optimise campaign performance  


🛠️ Tools & Technologies
________________________
- Excel – Data cleaning and preparation  
- SQL – Data integration and analysis  
- Tableau – Dashboard development and visualisation

- Customer demographics
